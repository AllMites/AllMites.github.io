# Cold Calling Strategy & Scripts

> Source: `call-script-taglish.md`, `WebDropshipping_Learnings.md`, `summer-web-business-learnings.md`, memory files

## Golden Rules

- Never say: mockup, GitHub, deploy, CNAME, domain
- Lead with OUTCOME, not product. Never "I build websites."
- Free preview = "gawa ako ng preview site na pwedeng pag-usapan natin"
- Speak slowly. Lolo/Lola pace.
- Smile while talking — changes tone.
- First 5 calls are warm-up. Don't judge by them.

## Call Timing

For Philippine micro-businesses: timing largely irrelevant. No gatekeepers — owner answers directly or doesn't.

**Don't call:** 8pm–9am (personal time), Sunday lunch (family time).

**Best window:** 2:00–4:00 PM (post-lunch lull, before evening rush).

**Key insight:** Businesses too busy to answer during peak hours are exactly the ones most likely to need a website. They'll call back.

## Three Buyer Types

### Type 1: "Wala akong paki / hindi kailangan"
**Signals:** "Hindi naman kailangan eh," "Busy ako," "Sige pag-isipan ko"

**Counter:** Lead with competitors. "Yung mga kakumpitensya n'yo — may website na. Kapag nag-search customer ng '[type] near me,' sila lumalabas. Kung wala kayo online, invisible kayo."

### Type 2: "Nasunog na ako sa ganyan"
**Signals:** "May gumawa na sa akin," "Nasayang lang pera ko"

**Counter:** Lead with free preview. "Kaya iba ginagawa ko — libre muna preview. Walang pera hangga't hindi n'yo gusto."

### Type 3: "Hindi ako marunong sa ganyan"
**Signals:** "Hindi ko alam 'yan," "Ano bang kailangan ko gawin?"

**Counter:** Lead with simplicity. "Ako na po bahala sa lahat. Ite-text ko na lang sa inyo link. Pindutin n'yo lang, makikita n'yo na sa phone."

## Opening Script (neutral — works for all types)

> "Magandang araw po, [business name] ito?"
> "Ako si [name]. Napansin ko lang po na wala kayong website — 'yung mga bagong customers, nagse-search kasi online bago pumunta sa isang lugar na tulad ng sa inyo."
> "Mayroon akong libreng preview — gagawa ako ng sample website para sa inyo sa susunod na 24 oras, para makita n'yo. Walang bayad, walang commitment."

## Staff / Non-Owner Answer

**Always include this branch in every script.** Expect it, don't fight it.

If staff/receptionist/employee answers:

> "Good afternoon po! Ask ko lang — kayo po ba ang owner or manager?"

→ If YES → proceed with normal pitch.
→ If NO:

> "Sige po, okay lang — pwede ko po ba malaman kung kailan mas okay tumawag ulit para sa owner or manager? Anong oras po sila usually nandito?"

**Then ask for callback time:**

- "Anong magandang oras tumawag ulit?"
- "Sige, text ko na lang din sa inyo yung details para maipakita n'yo sa kanila."

**Key rules:**
- Be polite — staff is your gateway, not your blocker
- Get a specific callback time (never "sige balik ka na lang")
- If they say "text mo na lang" — send a brief FB message, don't call blind again
- Don't pitch to staff — they can't decide. Just secure the next touchpoint
- If same staff answers again on follow-up call: "Kumusta po! Naipakita n'yo ba sa manager yung sinend ko?" — build rapport, don't restart cold

## Common Objections

**"Magkano?"**
"Sa ngayon, libre po preview. Pag nagustuhan n'yo, full website nasa ₱18,000. Pero tingnan n'yo muna preview."

**"Sige, pag-isipan ko."**
"Send-an ko na lang kayo sample link ngayong araw para makita n'yo itsura. Saan ko pwedeng i-send?"

**"Kailangan ko magtanong sa asawa/kasosyo/anak"**
"Puwede ko bang i-send sa inyo preview link para maipakita n'yo sa kanila?"

**"May Facebook na kami"**
"Iba kasi pag may website — lalabas kayo sa Google search. Yung naghahanap ng '[type] [area],' makikita kayo agad."

## After Yes — Info to Collect

1. Business name (exact)
2. Acceptable colors (or "bahala na ako")
3. Delivery method (Messenger, text, or Viber)

Do NOT ask for: photos, menu, detailed content. Build from what's online + placeholders.

## Three-Step Sequence (Call → DM → Call)

**Day 1 PM:** Cold call → anticipation + brief intro
**Day 2 AM:** DM with preview link → "Eto na po yung sabi ko"
**Day 2 PM:** Call with DM as context → "Na-check n'yo na po ba yung sinend ko kanina?"

This order matters: call first builds recognition, DM delivers the goods, second call closes the loop. Never DM before first call — the voice connection makes the DM land harder.

### DM (sent next morning after cold call)

> "Hi [shop name]! Ako po si [name], tumawag ako kahapon regarding sa sample website. Eto na po yung preview link: [URL]. Pindutin n'yo lang po, makikita n'yo agad sa phone ko po yan ginawa. Message lang po kayo kung may gusto baguhin!"

**Key details:**
- Reference the call ("tumawag ako kahapon") — anchors memory
- Deliver the link immediately — no "send ko mamaya," it's right there
- Tell them what to do ("pindutin n'yo lang po") — zero friction
- Offer to customize ("kung may gusto baguhin") — invites response

## Follow-up Text (send after call)

> "Hi [name]! Ako po si [your name], yung tumawag kanina. Salamat po! Gagawin ko preview sa susunod na 24 oras. I'll send the link dito pagtapos."

## Converting Preview → Paid

Wait 24-48 hours after sending preview. Follow up:
> "Hi [name]! Na-check n'yo po ba sinend ko? Kamusta naman? May gusto ba kayong baguhin?"
