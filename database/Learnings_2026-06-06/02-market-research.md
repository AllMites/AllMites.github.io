# Market Research — Parañaque, NCR

> Source: `WebDropshipping_Learnings.md`, `expanded-client-list.md`

## Geographic Scope

Start Parañaque (pitch refinement). Expand after first 1–2 closed deals. Higher-income areas (BGC, Makati) easier once you have portfolio proof. Remote delivery is 100% feasible — product is a URL.

| Vertical | Density | Price Tolerance | Build Order |

## Key Insight

Most viable businesses have zero web presence — Facebook only or directory listings only. This is the gap. A website makes them discoverable on Google where Facebook posts aren't.
