# Operator Context

> Source: `memory/operator-under-18.md`

## Identity

Operator is under 18. Affects interactions with older PH business owners but manageable through remote delivery model.

## Credibility Strategy

- **Portfolio does the selling** — age invisible behind professional design
- **Handle "ilang taon ka na?"** by redirecting to free preview: "17 po. Pero tingnan n'yo muna preview"
- **No face-to-face meetings** — video call or phone only
- **Remote delivery** — product is a URL, no reason to meet in person

## Contracting (Minor)

Minor can't form binding contracts. Contract-as-PDF-signature approach won't hold up legally.

**Workaround:**
- Use digital acceptance: text "agree" + GCash receipt as binding record
- Deal sizes (₱9,000 deposit) small enough that nobody will sue — they'll text if unhappy
- If formal contract needed, use email acceptance + payment receipt

## Communication

- Phone calls only — no in-person
- Taglish (Filipino + English mix) — natural for PH business owners
- Speak slowly, Lolo/Lola pace
- Portuguese accent may be a liability — practice Filipino delivery
