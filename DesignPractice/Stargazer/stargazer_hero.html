✩ Etymology  ·  Character
✩  SKYWARD SEEKING
Bred to look up when every other lily looked down. A flower of ambition, freckled with light.
✰
FRAGRANT BLOOM
Head-spinningly fragrant, it fills a room within minutes and holds court for over a week in the vase. Its scent — sweet, heady, impossible to ignore — is the calling card of a bloom that refuses subtlety. The flower of dreamers, bred for those who want to be remembered.
♢
IN THE LANGUAGE OF FLOWERS
Hanakotoba — the Japanese art of floral meaning — assigns the Stargazer one word: ambition. In Western floriography, it speaks of aspiration, prosperity, and a reach that exceeds the grasp. Give it to someone who dreams with their eyes open.
